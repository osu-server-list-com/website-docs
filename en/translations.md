osu!Server List is a community-driven platform, and we need your help to make it accessible to players worldwide. Whether you're fluent in multiple languages or just want to contribute to your native language, your help is invaluable!

* * *

##### Why Contribute?

By contributing translations, you help:

- **Expand Global Reach**: Make the platform accessible to non-English speaking communities
- **Improve User Experience**: Allow players to use the site in their preferred language
- **Build Community**: Connect with other translators and contributors worldwide
- **Support Open Source**: Be part of an open-source project that benefits thousands of players

* * *

* * *

##### How to Get Started

1. **Choose a Repository**: Select either website translations or documentation (or both!)
2. **Fork the Repository**: Create your own copy to work on
3. **Find Your Language**: Check if your language exists, or create a new language file
4. **Translate**: Add or improve translations following the existing format
5. **Submit a Pull Request**: Once you're done, submit your changes for review
6. **Get Reviewed**: Our team will review and merge your contributions

* * *

##### Translation Guidelines

To maintain consistency and quality across all translations:

- **Keep the Tone Casual**: Match the friendly, community-focused tone of the original English text
- **Preserve Formatting**: Keep all formatting tags, variables, and placeholders intact
- **Stay Accurate**: Translate the meaning, not just word-for-word
- **Use Gaming Terms**: Use commonly accepted gaming terminology in your language
- **Ask Questions**: If something is unclear, open an issue on GitHub or ask in Discord
